## Interpretation

v24 is a micro-neighborhood search around the v23 winner. The main question is whether the coarse `0.5` task-grad weight was already the real local optimum, or whether a slightly smaller/larger weight or a tiny query residual gives a cleaner tradeoff.

- Final recommendation: `VT-0.5`
- Notes: VT-0.375 and VT-0.5 stayed exactly tied on Core-XL, T1-XL, T2a-XL, and fresh T1-R reruns. Keep VT-0.5 as the simpler default because the local neighborhood is flat rather than because it showed a measurable aggregate win.
- Conclusion: The micro-neighborhood is flat. `VT-0.375` and `VT-0.5` tied exactly on `Core-XL`, `T1-XL`, `T2a-XL`, and the paired high-batch `T1-R` reruns. `VT-0.625` and `VT-0.5+Q0.125` fell behind in screening and never recovered. The practical conclusion is to keep `VT-0.5` as the default because it is the simpler representative of a flat optimum, not because it achieved a unique measured win.
